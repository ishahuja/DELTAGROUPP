DELTA

Delta is a puzzle-platformer horror adventure game that is intended to be a form of expression which has no true conclusion, but a prompt termination at the end revealing the real truth.


The initial environment is set to a dystopian world. When the player touches certain objects, the world around him glitches into a different environment - a peaceful nature setting. The player will face enemies and obstacles during their journey. The abrupt ending of the game will reveal that the world in which the character exists is a simulation, a matrix, with no further explanation, leaving a lot of questions unanswered. It is our goal to grant the Player the ability to have their own interpretation of the game , there is no right or wrong one. This will open the world of game theories created by Players, attracting even more interest to the game itself.
